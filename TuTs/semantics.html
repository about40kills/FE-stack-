WWF

The World Wide Fund for nature(WWF) is an international organization working on issues regarding the conservation, research, and preservation of the environment, formally named the World Wildlife Fund.WWF was founded in 1961.

WWF's Panda Symbol

The Panda has become the symbol of the WWF.The well-known panda logo of WWF originated from a panda named Chi Chi that was transferred from the Beijing zoo to London zoo in the same year of establishment of the WWF.
